Scrambled Eggs

Description

Scrambled eggs are a quick and versatile breakfast dish made by beating eggs and cooking them gently in a pan. They can be seasoned simply with salt and pepper or enhanced with butter, milk, or cheese for a richer flavor and texture.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Crack eggs into a bowl and beat until fully mixed.
  2. Heat butter in a nonstick skillet over medium heat.
  3. Pour in the eggs and let them set slightly.
  4. Gently stir with a spatula until soft curds form.
  5. Remove from heat while slightly undercooked; they will finish with residual heat. Season and serve.
