PB&J

Description

A peanut butter and jelly (PB&J) sandwich is a classic American snack or meal consisting of two slices of bread spread with peanut butter and fruit jelly or jam. The peanut butter provides a creamy or crunchy texture with a rich, nutty flavor, while the jelly adds sweetness and moisture, typically made from grape, strawberry, or other fruit preserves. This combination creates a balance of salty and sweet tastes, and is often served cold or at room temperature. PB&J is simple, affordable, and widely popular, especially among children.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 tablespoons peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons fruit jelly or jam (e.g., grape, strawberry)

Steps

  1. Lay the two slices of bread flat on a clean surface.
  2. Spread peanut butter evenly on one slice.
  3. Spread jelly or jam evenly on the other slice.
  4. Press the two slices together, spreads facing inward.
  5. Slice in half if desired; serve immediately.
